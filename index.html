How to Play

Welcome to EXPLAIN, where your goal is to help an AI guess the Challenge Word. The challenge — you need to do it as efficiently as possible. The fewer characters you use, the better your score.

There are four game modes, each offering a different way to play:

Daily Challenge: A new word every day. You get only 5 attempts, so make every clue count!

Shuffle Mode: Play random puzzles with unlimited attempts! Experiment and refine your strategy without restriction.

Custom Mode: Challenge the AI to guess a word of your choosing, with unlimited attempts!

Archive Mode: Set high scores on all previous daily puzzles, dating back to February 1, 2025!

How It Works:

In every game, the AI has a Challenge Word it needs to guess, with the help of your clues. These can be single words, short phrases, or whatever you'd like that nudges the AI in the right direction. The AI relies on associations, logical connections, etc. — so think carefully before submitting your clue.

All clues must be 25 characters or fewer, but the best clues are short, meaningful, and specific. Precise clues help the AI eliminate wrong guesses quickly, while shorter clues increase your chance of getting a better score.

Metrics

As you play, you’ll see four key indicators of your progress:

Confidence Score

After each guess, the AI provides a Confidence Score (0% – 100%), indicating how sure it is about its answer. High confidence suggests the AI thinks it is close with its guess, while low confidence indicates it's taking a shot in the dark. A high confidence level doesn’t always mean the AI is close, but it does mean your clue is leading it in a strong direction.

Search Space

Search Space represents the number of possible words the AI is considering based on your clue. A broad clue results in a large search space (many possible words), while a well-targeted clue shrinks the search space to just a few possibilities. Ideally, you want to reduce the Search Space while keeping the AI confident in its guess. This becomes increasingly difficult with fewer characters!

Character Usage & Attempts

In Daily Challenge mode, you have 5 attempts to get the AI to guess the correct word, and your first successful Clue is locked in as your answer. The best players are able to get the AI to guess the answer in very few characters. If you use short, precise clues, you’ll achieve a better score and rank higher on the leaderboard!

Winning & Losing

If you run out of attempts on the Daily Challenge, that's okay - you can try again tomorrow! If the AI correctly guesses the word on one of your five attempts, you win!

All Other Game Modes: No limit on attempts! Keep playing and refining your strategy.

Each day is a new challenge! Think strategically, give your best clues, and leave the rest to the AI.
